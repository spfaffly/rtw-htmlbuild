  • Patrick Kelley • www.pk-worldwide.com
Previous Next

Each featured photographer donates a print, and River to Well designates 100% of the proceeds (less shipping) to bring clean water to those who need it. Their talent earns awards and their generosity helps fund ongoing water projects in Africa.

  • YOU donate $55.
  • We MAIL the print to you.
  • Wells in Africa are built and restored.

With a $55 donation RTW, in partnership with the photographer, we will mail you a print that measures 10” wide with a maintained aspect ratio. In our experience, it costs about $5 to print and ship, so essentially $50 of your $55 goes toward the well.
