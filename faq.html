Frequently Asked Question's

Why a photo competition?
Photo competitions foster an atmosphere of creativity, pride and camaraderie -- it gives artists an outlet to do good while showcasing their best work.
Where does the money from my submission(s) go?
There are regions all over the world that need clean water and better sanitation; we focus on sub-Saharan Africa. Our partner, VOX United, is an international nonprofit that has sustainable, long-term relationships with people and communities there. As we identify specific projects, we publish these details so you know exactly how your donations are being used.
How much of every dollar goes toward clean water projects?
One hundred percent of all entry fees go toward the direct costs associated with clean water projects, including, but not limited to, well construction, well maintenance, water filter installation and education.
How much does it cost to build a well?
Wells cost anywhere between $3,000 to $6,000. Depending on how deep the contractors have to dig, the costs could increase; deeper wells cost more money.
Who is responsible for the construction associated with RTW-funded projects?
We always want local businesses investing in their communities. We work exclusively with VOX United and partner with locals in order to implement solutions and technologies appropriate to their geographic region and culture.

Are there any events associated with the River to Well Photo Competition?
Yes. In the fall of 2012, River to Well will exhibit winning photos in Lawrence, Kansas; Kansas City, Missouri; Denver, Colorado; and Minneapolis, Minnesota. Be sure to check the Event page for complete details.
How will I know if I am a winner?
After the River to Well Judging Committee has completed all judging, each contestant will be notified by email that she or he has or has not won. Winners will receive instructions on when and where to pick up prizes.
Will you return my photo after the competition?
River to Well keeps all submissions. If you have specific questions, please refer to the Official Rules on the Submit page or email us at info@rivertowell.com
Who put this all together?
The RTW Team is comprised of a group of friends who have a passion for art and a deep love for people. We want to do good, change lives, build wells and encourage others to join us in the fight for clean water.
